Biography

The inspiration for this website is drawn from my love of cinematic photography . I have always been fascinated by the way in which a single frame can tell a story. my faourite photographer is Alex Carter and I have always been inspired by his work. I hope to one day be able to capture the world in the same way that he does. It's amazing to see other people's perspective of the world through a camera lens.
